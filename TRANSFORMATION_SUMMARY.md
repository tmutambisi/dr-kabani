# Website Transformation Summary

This website has been transformed from a basic template into a **strategic platform** designed to support authority, legacy, and revenue scaling for Dr. Daniel Ngombo Kabani.

## Key Changes Implemented

### 1. **Removed All Traces of "Lovable" Branding**
- ✅ Updated `README.md` - Removed all Lovable references and created professional project documentation
- ✅ Removed `lovable-tagger` dependency from `package.json`
- ✅ Cleaned up `vite.config.ts` - Removed lovable-tagger import and usage
- ✅ Uninstalled lovable-tagger package via npm

### 2. **Enhanced Hero Section - Authority Positioning**
**Before:** "Empowering lives, one conversation at a time"
**After:** "Mastery in Business. Depth in Purpose."

**Changes:**
- Badge updated to: "Authority • Legacy • Excellence"
- Repositioned as "Strategic Authority" bridging healthcare and business
- Updated credentials: "Global Speaker • Strategic Advisor • Award-Winning Author"
- More authoritative, less conversational tone

### 3. **Created Multi-Tier Services Section**
Added a new `ServicesSection.tsx` component showcasing three distinct offer tiers:

#### **Tier 1: Insight & Knowledge** (Mass Market)
- Target: Individuals
- Offerings: Books, articles, digital resources, personal development tools
- CTA: "Explore Library"

#### **Tier 2: Empowerment & Training** (Mid-Market)
- Target: Teams & Organizations
- Offerings: Keynote speaking, corporate workshops, team building, capacity building
- CTA: "Book Dr. Kabani"
- **Featured/Highlighted tier**

#### **Tier 3: Strategic Advisory** (Elite)
- Target: Executives & Corporations
- Offerings: Executive coaching, board advisory, strategic consulting, organizational design
- CTA: "Inquire Now"

### 4. **Navigation Updates**
Added "Services" to the main navigation menu, positioned strategically between "About" and "Speaking"

### 5. **Design Philosophy**
The website now embodies:
- **Authority & Credibility** - Professional positioning as a thought leader
- **Legacy Building** - Emphasis on long-term impact and transformation
- **Revenue Scalability** - Clear pathways from free content to premium services
- **Strategic Depth** - Moving beyond "motivational" to "transformational"
- **Premium Positioning** - Comparable to world-class business coaches and thought leaders
